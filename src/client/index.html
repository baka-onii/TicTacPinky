Tic Tac Pinky

Ultimate Tic-Tac-Toe

Play Tic Tac Pinky

A Tic-Tac-Toe game reimagined — 9 mini-boards in one. Choose how to play.

  • The board is a 3×3 grid of mini-boards, each itself a 3×3 grid.
  • The cell you pick sends your opponent to the mini-board in the corresponding position.
  • Win a mini-board (3-in-a-row) to claim that cell of the large board.
  • Win three mini-boards in a row on the large board to win the game.
  • If your move would send your opponent to a mini-board that's already decided, they may play anywhere.
